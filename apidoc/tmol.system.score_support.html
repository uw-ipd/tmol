Constructor parameters for torsion space scoring.

Extract constructor kwargs to initialize a KinematicAtomicCoordinateProvider and BondedAtomScoreGraph subclass supporting torsion-space scoring. This includes only bond_torsion dofs, a subset of valid kinematic dofs for the system.
