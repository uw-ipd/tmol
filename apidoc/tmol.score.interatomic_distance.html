Base graph for interatomic distances.

Graph component calculating interatomic distances. Distances are present once in the source graph for a given atomic pair; the rendered distances are equivalent to the upper triangle of the full interatomic distance matrix.

Distances are rendered as two tensor properties, atom_pair_inds and atom_pair_dist, containing the pair of atomic indicies in coords and the calculated distance respectively.

Components requiring access to interatomic distance components must make the component’s interatomic threshold distance available by implementing the component_atom_pair_dist_threshold property. The generated interatomic distance graph will respecte the maximum required interatomic distance of all score graph components.
